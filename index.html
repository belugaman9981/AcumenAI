Our belief · AcumenAI

Intelligence should belong to the person using it —
not a server farm in Virginia.

AcumenAI was built out of frustration with platforms that phone home, train on your data, and keep the results to themselves. A model that runs entirely on your hardware is fundamentally more trustworthy — and fundamentally more yours.

Our evolutionary brain is built on small-population neural evolution: a method that compounds prediction accuracy across generations, seeded by the world's largest open knowledge base, and constrained only by iteration speed and the CPU in front of you.

What we stand for

Built on a few hard principles.
We make choices that let you assume these. They're the right solutions anyway.

01

Your data stays yours

Everything — conversations, brain state, training data — lives on your machine. We never see it. No telemetry, no silent uploads.

02

Open by default

The full source is on GitHub with an MIT license. Fork it, modify it, run it on air-gapped hardware. We don't mind.

03

Continuous learning

The brain keeps evolving in the background while you chat. Leave it running overnight and come back to a smarter agent.

04

No subscription required

One download, zero recurring cost. Run it on your $5000 laptop or your rack server. AcumenAI doesn't charge you to think.

05

Hardware-accessible

Runs on Python 3.11+ with pure pip dependencies. No CUDA, no containers, no exotic setup — just clone and go.

06

Research first

Every design decision traces back to a real outcome. We'd rather be the most curious than the most capitalized.
